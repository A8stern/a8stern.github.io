PDF Scanner App - FAQ

Find answers to frequently asked questions about our PDF Scanner app.

1. How do I set a password for my documents?

You can set a password during the app setup. If you wish to change it later, go to the Settings screen.

2. How do I access all my documents?

After entering the correct password, you will be taken to the screen that lists all your securely stored documents.

3. Can I delete a document once it's scanned?

Yes, you can delete any document by selecting it and choosing the delete option from the available actions.

4. How do I create a new document?

On the main document screen, tap the “+” button to access the scanner and create a new document.

5. How do I add pages to an existing document?

Open the document, then use the scanner option to add more pages.

6. How can I share my scanned documents?

Open the document and use the share option to send it via email, cloud services, or other apps.

7. How do I delete a single page from a document?

While viewing the document, select the page you want to delete and confirm the action.

8. How do I access the scanner?

Press the “+” button from the main screen or within a document to open the scanner.

9. Can I change my password?

Yes, go to the Settings screen where you can change your password and adjust other security options.

10. Can I display the password while typing?

In the Settings screen, you can toggle the option to show or hide the password while typing.

11. How do I subscribe to premium features?

Go to the Settings screen and tap on the Subscription option to view and purchase plans.

12. How do I request a refund?

Visit the Subscription screen in the app and tap “Request a Refund” for assistance.

13. How can I restore my subscription if I change devices?

On the Subscription screen, there is an option to restore your purchase if you switch to a new device.

14. What happens if I forget my password?

If you forget your password, you may need to reset the app and lose access to previously stored documents. Always keep your password safe!

15. Can I use the app without subscribing?

Yes, basic scanning and document management features are available without a subscription. Premium features require a subscription.
